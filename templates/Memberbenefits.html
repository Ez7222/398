MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

At RGSQ, we’re more than just a society – we’re a vibrant community of people who share an interest in geography and in exploring our natural and built environments. As a member, you will unlock a world of opportunities:

Join RGSQ today

Discounts and Savings

Enjoy early access and discounted rates for all our Events and Activities. These include lectures, discussion panels and trips, focussing on a wide range of topics and places.

Local, National and Overseas Trips for Members Only

Join unique local, national, and overseas trips available exclusively to RGSQ members. See where we’ve been in our Past Events.

Access to Resources

Dive into our extensive Library housing over 3,000 books and a growing collection of journals and maps. Borrowing is a members-only privilege.

Showcasing Your Creativity

Enter the members’ only Photo Competition and win great prizes & bragging rights! See past entries here.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join projects, events or committees – numerous ways to contribute your skills and expertise.

Members Forum

Join our Members Forum to exchange ideas, resources and collaborate.
