Membership Benefits

At RGSQ, we’re more than just a society - we’re a vibrant community of people who share an interest in geography and in exploring our natural and built environments. As a member, you will unlock a world of opportunities:

Join RGSQ Today
Discounts and Savings

Enjoy early access and discounted rates for all our events and activities. These include lectures, discussion panels and trips, focussing on a wide range of topics and places.

Local, National and Overseas Trips for Members Only

Join unique local, national, and overseas trips available exclusively to RGSQ members. Immerse yourself in enriching experiences that deepen your understanding of our planet’s geography and cultures. See where we have been in our Past Events. (Although some local trips are open to non-members at extra cost, most are booked out by members.)

Access to Resources

Dive into our extensive Library housing over 3,000 books and a growing collection of journals and maps. Borrowing books and journals from our library is a members’ only privilege.

Showcasing Your Creativity

Enter the members' only Photo Competition, designed to showcase members’ creative talents. Capture and share your best photographic work on a set topic, with opportunities to win great prizes and bragging rights – it’s also fun! To look at past entries visit RGSQ Photo Competition.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering with RGSQ offers opportunities for members to contribute their skills and expertise. Whether you're interested in joining a committee, assisting with specific projects, or supporting events and and activities, there are numerous ways to be involved.

Members Forum

Engage in our Members Forum, where you can exchange ideas, discuss topics of interest, share resources, and collaborate with your fellow members.
