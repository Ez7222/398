The Queensland Science Network (QSN) is a collaboration between Queensland’s science societies to promote science events and activities and to share knowledge. QSN is hosted by The Royal Society of Queensland, the State’s oldest learned academy. RGSQ is one of the twenty seven member organisations and we encourage our members to visit the QSN website and subscribe to their informative Newsletter from the QSN home page.
