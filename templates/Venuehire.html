Venue Hire

Conveniently located in Spring Hill (just 4km from Brisbane city), the Royal Geographical Society of Queensland has two spaces available for casual or long-term hire: 1) a lecture/meeting area for large meetings & presentations, and 2) a boardroom.

Large Meeting Room For Hire

Sitting Capacity: 50 peoples

Location: Gregory Place, Level 1, 28 Fortescue Street Spring Hill, Qld, 4000

Parking Street Parking and many options for Public transport available.

Facilities include:

  • Air conditioning
  • Access to a kitchenette with
    • Crockery - cups, saucers, plates, spoons, knives, forks, wine and water/juice glasses
    • Dishwasher
    • Microwave
    • Refrigerator
    • BYO or external food catering
  • Private toilets (including with wheelchair access)
  • Audio system - roving and lapel microphone available
  • Video projector
  • White board
  • Internet access
  • No alcohol permitted

Fees* Meeting/Lecture Room Hire: $115.00 incl GST per 1-4 hours; $220.00 incl GST per 8 hours;

Boardroom for Hire

Sitting Capacity: 12 Persons

Location: As Above

Facilities: as above, including projecting capabilities

Fees* Boardroom Hire: $60.00 incl GST per 1-4 hours; $115.00 incl GST per 8 hours;

A cleaning fee may be imposed if extra cleaning is required after the meeting room or boardroom hire.

Booking

Please contact the RGSQ Office at info@rgsq.org.au or call 07 3368 2066 to discuss availability, bookings and hire agreement.

The spaces are generally available Monday to Friday from 09:00 to 18:00; weekend hire can be arranged.

*Please note prices are reviewed annually; the displayed prices are valid as at 24/09/2024.
