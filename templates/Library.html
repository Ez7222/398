LIBRARY

The library is cataloged in the

RGSQ eHive Library

The eHive online catalogue contains all the books held in the RGSQ library. The Collections Committee is in the process of adding its maps and journals to the catalogue.

The public can access RGSQ’s catalogue through the National Library of Australia TROVE system as well as through eHive.

People wanting to view the physical items in RGSQ’s collections should make an appointment through RGSQ reception at 07 3368 2066.
