Donate

The Royal Geographical Society of Queensland is a voluntary, not-for-profit organisation that promotes and encourages a greater understanding of the world around us.

To achieve its mission, the Society operates the RGSQ Environmental Fund for the specific purpose of supporting dissemination of information, provision of education, and conduct of research about the environment. Donations to the RGSQ Environmental Fund are tax deductible.
