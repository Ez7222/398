The Royal Geographical Society of Queensland Ltd. (RGSQ)

Advancing geographical science, and promoting public awareness and enjoyment of geography in Queensland and across Australia.

The Society is a voluntary, not-for-profit organisation that brings together people who share an interest in geography, places, and the environment. Our activities include lectures, day trips and longer treks, active research and fieldwork, and support for geographic education.
